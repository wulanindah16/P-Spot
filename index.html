About

P-SPOT (Piezoelectric Site Prediction and Optimization Tool) is a geospatial-based technology designed to determine the most optimal locations for installing piezoelectric modules, which are devices that can convert pressure or vibrations from footsteps into electrical energy. This system utilizes GeoAI-powered artificial intelligence with Swarm Optimization algorithms to effectively analyze spatial factors such as pedestrian traffic intensity and surrounding environmental conditions, based on data-driven insights.

P-SPOT was developed as a solution to the growing demand for clean energy amid the high dependence on fossil fuels. Through this innovative approach, mechanical energy generated from human footsteps can be transformed into sustainable and environmentally friendly electrical energy. The energy produced can be used for street lighting, charging stations, and small-scale electrical systems in urban areas. In this way, every human step becomes a tangible contribution to achieving energy efficiency, reducing carbon emissions, and creating smart, future-oriented cities.

What is Piezoelectric Technology?

Piezoelectric is a material that can generate electrical energy when subjected to pressure or vibration. When a person steps on a surface equipped with a piezoelectric module, the mechanical energy from each footstep is converted into electrical energy that can be continuously reused. Piezoelectric has the characteristic that greater pressure generates greater power.

The working principle of piezoelectric technology is relatively simple. The pressure from footsteps causes a change in electrical charge within the crystal structure of the piezoelectric material. This change produces an unstable alternating current (AC), which is then directed to a rectifier circuit to be converted into a more stable direct current (DC). The generated electricity is stored in a battery or capacitor so it can be used for various low-power electronic devices.

A single human step can generate up to 0.035 watts of electrical energy, enough to power a small LED light with just a few steps. When applied in areas with high pedestrian traffic, this technology can produce significant amounts of energy for street lighting or other simple electrical needs, while supporting the development of efficient, environmentally friendly, and sustainable clean energy innovations.

Impact and Benefits

Renewable Energy

Piezoelectric technology enables the conversion of kinetic energy from human footsteps into clean electricity. Although a single step only generates up to 0.035 watts, when applied in an area with around 10,000 steps per day, the system can produce approximately 350 watts of energy daily, enough to power several LED lights. By utilizing energy that would otherwise be wasted, this technology helps reduce dependence on fossil fuels while supporting the diversification of environmentally friendly energy sources.

Environmentally Friendly

Piezoelectric systems produce no direct carbon emissions, as they harness energy from human activity without relying on fossil fuels. Based on GeoAI analysis, this technology has the potential to save around 4,731 grams of CO₂ per day in areas with high pedestrian traffic, demonstrating a tangible contribution to reducing emissions. It serves as a source of green and eco-friendly energy while raising awareness of the importance of sustainable energy use.

Smart City

The implementation of piezoelectric technology supports the concept of a smart city through the provision of independent and environmentally friendly energy. When applied in areas with high pedestrian activity, this system can generate local energy for public street lighting and charging stations. With efficient and sustainable energy utilization, this technology contributes to the creation of adaptive, energy-efficient, and sustainability-oriented cities.
